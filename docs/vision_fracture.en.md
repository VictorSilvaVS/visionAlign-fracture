# VisionFracture and BodyMaker Identification (BM ID)

VisionFracture operates in an integrated manner with VisionAlign. While VisionAlign identifies the presence and global positioning of the can, it isolates and delivers a specific **Region of Interest (ROI)** for VisionFracture to process with high pixel density.

## Fracture and BM ID Detection
VisionFracture specializes in two critical tasks that occur simultaneously within the received ROI:
1.  **Structural Integrity:** Identification of fractures, fissures, or deformations on the can's rim and body.
2.  **BodyMaker Identification (BM ID):** Reading the identification code of the machine that produced the can (*BodyMaker Identification*), essential for industrial traceability.
