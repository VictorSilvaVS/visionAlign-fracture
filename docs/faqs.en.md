# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

This section explains, in a simple and direct way, how the VisionSystem intelligence helps your factory produce with higher quality and less waste.

---

### How do the two systems work together?
Imagine an inspection team where everyone has a specialty:
*   **VisionAlign (The Scout):** Quickly locates the can on the conveyor belt, ignoring vibrations or misalignments.
*   **VisionFracture (The Inspector):** Receives the area isolated by the scout and focuses exclusively on finding micro-fissures and reading the machine code.

### How does the system read the BM ID under industrial conditions?
VisionSystem solves the challenge of reading codes on metal (even with reflections and oil) in two steps:
*   **Precision Focus:** The system isolates only the code area (ROI), creating a "clean" image for analysis.
*   **Geometric Vision:** The AI reconstructs the engraving reliefs, allowing reading even if the code is faint or covered in excess lubricant.

### How can the AI distinguish similar letters (like "I" and "i")?
Unlike a regular scanner, the AI analyzes the anatomy of the letter:
*   **Topology:** Identifies the dot on the lowercase "i" and the straight structure of the uppercase "I".
*   **Geometry:** Evaluates the height and width ratio of each character.
*   **Context:** Understands your factory's engraving pattern to avoid statistical confusion.

### Is 90% accuracy good? Why does the system take time to update?
In the industry, **90% superiority** is an extremely high level of excellence:
*   **Maximum Safety:** The system only changes the "brain" if the new model proves to be significantly more reliable than the current one.
*   **Rigorous Validation:** We test the new model against hundreds of real photos (30% of the database) before any changes.
*   **Guaranteed Backup:** The previous model is never deleted; it is kept as a safety reserve for instant rollback.

### How does the system get smarter on its own?
The system works like an employee who specializes every day:
*   **Uncertainty Capture:** Every time the AI finds something new or doubtful, it saves that image.
*   **Self-Adjustment:** The OTX engine studies these new images and adjusts the model automatically.
*   **Evolution Without a Programmer:** The AI learns new defect variations without you needing to hire technical support for it.

### Do oil or dirt on the can hinder the system?
No, as we trained the intelligence to be "immune" to visual noise:
*   **Selective Vision:** The AI ignores stains, reflections, and smudges.
*   **Structural Focus:** The system focuses only on the actual metal deformation or the physical shape of the engraved character.

### Is the system secure? Who can access my data?
Security is treated with an industrial standard:
*   **Edge Computing:** All processing is local. Photos do not leave your factory.
*   **Total Privacy:** No mandatory connection to the cloud or internet.
*   **Access Control:** The dashboard is restricted to your internal industrial network.

### Are the libraries used paid?
There are no license costs for the intelligence engine:
*   **Intel Technology:** We use OpenVINO and OTX, which are free and open-source tools.
*   **No Monthly Fees:** You don't pay fees for using artificial intelligence.
*   **Auditable System:** The libraries are safe, transparent, and do not transmit data to third parties.
